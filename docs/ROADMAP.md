# TradePilot Roadmap

## MVP

- Tenant-aware auth
- Broker account model
- Paper execution engine
- Trade planner
- Risk rules
- Basic analytics: P&L, win rate, drawdown, expectancy
- Dhan, Zerodha, Delta adapters

## Phase 2

- Angel One adapter
- Real-time market and order streams
- Shared workspaces and roles
- Attachments and screenshots
- Rule-violation tracking

## Phase 3

- AI advisory layer
- Natural-language analytics
- Coaching summaries
- SaaS billing and subscription controls
